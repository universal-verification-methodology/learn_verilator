# Metrics that matter

Simulation without metrics is hope

---

## Pass rate, coverage, escapes
- Pass rate tells you how many tests succeed right now, one flaky fail blocks confidence
- Coverage percent tells you how much of the intended space you exercised
- Bug escapes are the painful teacher

## Real Verilator practice
- In Track A, run a small regression, even two tests, and record pass/fail in notes
- If you have a coverage switch or simple checklist from EXAMPLES
- Name one hypothetical escape and what test would catch it
- This is literacy, not a full coverage closure project

---

## Pitfalls to watch
- Do not celebrate hundred percent pass rate with zero coverage depth
- They teach the vocabulary
- And do not sign off when status is BLOCKED; fix the escape class or add tests first
